Parent Resources

We look forward to working with you and your boys as they join Troop 22 for their scouting ventures. Below is some information on how to get started with the troop. We hope this is a positive experience for you and your boy(s).

To get started in joining the troop, we will need to start with a completed Scouts BSA Youth Application and a BSA Medical Form (Parts A and B only, not C). Review initial Scouts BSA fees here.

Once the application is completed, you can head to the local Scout Shop in Orem to pick up a full Scouts BSA uniform with the Crossroads of the West Council patch and the troop numerals “22”. The Scout Shop does offer sewing with a fee of the patches. An alternative source may be to search the local Deseret Industries stores for used uniforms.

For more information, head to our What To Expect page for additional information. If you have any additional questions, you may email Rod Hunsaker or any member of Troop 22 committee.
